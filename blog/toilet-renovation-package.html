Overview of Toilet Renovation Packages in Singapore

Toilet renovation packages in Singapore provide homeowners with fixed-price bundles that cover essentials such as tiling, waterproofing, plumbing fixtures, carpentry, and installation. These packages help manage renovation budgets while keeping the costs predictable and transparent.

Package prices differ based on layout size, tile choice, sanitary fittings, and whether the unit is an HDB or condominium. Premium options include higher-end materials, concealed piping, and custom vanity designs.

What Is Included in a Toilet Renovation Package?

While inclusions differ among renovation companies, most packages cover the following:

  • Hacking & disposal of old wall and floor tiles.
  • Waterproofing for wet areas.
  • Floor and wall tiling with homeowner-chosen designs.
  • Plumbing installation for WC, basin, and shower components.
  • New sanitary fittings such as tap, basin, and toilet bowl.
  • Aluminium shower screen or tempered glass partition.
  • Lighting and basic electrical work such as heater installation.
  • Vanity cabinet for storage and design enhancement.
