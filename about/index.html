About FreePropertySG

Singapore’s fully free property listing platform for everyone.

Our Mission

FreePropertySG was created with a simple goal — to make property browsing and posting free, fast, and accessible for everyone in Singapore.
We believe finding a home should not require paying listing fees or complicated commissions.

What We Offer

  • ✔ Free HDB, Condo, and Landed property listings
  • ✔ No account required to browse
  • ✔ No listing fees — ever
  • ✔ Simple, clean, distraction-free browsing
  • ✔ Direct contact between buyers, sellers, and tenants

Why We Built This

Many property seekers and sellers prefer a simple platform without agent obligations or hidden charges.
FreePropertySG provides a clean experience focused only on listings — nothing else.
